# Community Insights — What Actually Works

## Biggest Wins Reported

1. **Trojan Loom outreach** — 25 custom videos/day, under 2 mins each, shows their actual GMB/website, calls next day as warm follow-up. Went from 35 no-shows to closing 1-2 clients/day.
2. **Facebook local business group posts** — free, warm leads come to you
3. **Cold SMS** — higher volume, less emotional toll than cold calling
4. **Image ads at $35/day** — closed first client in one week with no sales call
5. **VSL pre-selling** — prospects show up saying "I already decided, let's go"
6. **Speed to action** — people who start outreach before finishing course get clients faster

## Biggest Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Free trials** — never do it. Client gets free work then disappears.
2. **Starting at $97** — these clients are most demanding and least appreciative. Lead with $297, downsell to $197.
3. **Not walking client through checkout on the call** — they disappear if you send a link and hope
4. **Doing cold SMS in same account as agency** — phone number gets flagged
5. **Offering free trials without card on file**
6. **Tech-challenged clients** — disproportionate onboarding time. Screen for this early.
7. **Barber shops** — Booksy already handles their needs, impossible sell

## A2P Registration (SMS Compliance)

- Biggest technical headache in the community
- Use toll-free numbers to skip A2P entirely
- If using local numbers, hire a VA with A2P experience
- Canadian members face extra hurdles

## Stripe Warning

- Stripe freezes accounts on sudden revenue spikes
- Have Whop or NMI as backup payment processor
- Never rely on Stripe alone

## Show Rate Best Practices

- Cap booking window to 48-72 hours
- Call immediately when they book, again morning of
- Thank you page with short video + Add to Calendar button
- Use Google Meet or phone — not Zoom (too much friction for tradies)
- Frame it as "strategy session" not "demo"
- If no-show confirmed 24hrs out: double-book the slot
