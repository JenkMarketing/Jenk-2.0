# Cold SMS System

## The Two Sequences

### Sequence 1 — Free Website Incentive
Message 1: "Hey [name], this is Michael. I was looking you guys up on Google and saw that you don't have a website or anything like that. I know this is kind of random, but I went ahead and built you one. Do you want to see it? Just say bye-bye if you want me to stop."

Follow up 1 (54 mins later): "Hey did you see my last message?"

Follow up 2 (1 day later): "Hey, don't want to be hassled, but if seeing the awesome website made for you doesn't sound bad, let me know and I'll show you how. If not, all good."

### Sequence 2 — Leads Incentive
Message 1: "Hey [name], this is Michael. I'm looking for one company that can handle an extra 5 to 10-15 jobs in the next 8 weeks. Are you able to take that on?"

Follow up 1: "Hey did you see my last message?"

Follow up 2: "Hey, if adding 8-20k in profit doesn't sound bad, let me know. I'll show you how. If not, all good."

## Pipeline Stages

- New (just imported)
- Replied (any reply)
- Positive (wants to know more)
- Not Interested (told us to stop)
- Called (we called them)
- Booked (appointment set)
- No Show
- Closed

## Positive Reply Signals

"who's this", "sure", "yeah", "show me", "interested", "tell me more", "how much", "what is it", "sounds good", "maybe", "new phone"

## Negative Reply Signals

"stop", "not interested", "remove me", "unsubscribe", "no thanks", "leave me alone", "f*** off", "don't text"

## Key Rules

- Only text between 9am and 7pm
- Max 60 contacts per hour
- Always give opt-out option
- Use separate sub-account — never mix with client accounts
- Toll-free numbers to avoid A2P issues
- 500 contacts at a time = ~2 days of responses

## Call Script After Positive Reply

"Hey what's up [name], it's Michael — I'm the guy who literally sent you a message asking if you could handle a couple more jobs, I don't know if that rings a bell. Yeah no worries at all, but are you guys still taking on more work or are you pretty booked out right now? [listen] Cool — so basically I work specifically with [niche] businesses and I help them get more jobs organically so they don't have to rely on HomeAdvisor or Thumbtack. I don't want to waste too much of your time — if you've got 10-15 mins today or tomorrow to jump on a quick Zoom I can show you exactly what it looks like. Is that fair?"
