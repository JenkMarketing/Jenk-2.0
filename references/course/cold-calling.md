# Cold Calling System

## The Script Framework

**Opening:** "Hey is this [name/business]?"

**Hook:** "My mum's friend Julie Becker referred me — she said you do super high quality work, you're the [niche] right?"

**Pivot:** "She mentioned you don't have a website/[service] — I just graduated and started a little agency, I'm doing this for super cheap hoping to get a testimonial. Do you mind if I build something out for you to take a look at?"

**Price anchor:** "$97-497/month depending on how involved you want to get"

**Book:** "I have [time] today or [time] tomorrow — which works better?"

## Key Mindset Rules

- Goal #1 is just to not get hung up on — "getting in conversation" is a win
- Sound like a real person, not a script reader
- Most closes happen on call 5-11 — follow up relentlessly
- There is NO downside to cold calling, you can only get better
- Do 30-50 calls just to get comfortable before trying to book anything

## Top Objections

**"We already have enough business"** → "Totally understand — the reason this still works is it lets you charge MORE for the same jobs. Quote overflow at a higher price — if they say no you're in the same place, if they say yes you made money you otherwise wouldn't have."

**"Too expensive"** → Hit them with the math. If they charge $2-5K per job, one extra job per year = 10-40x return on $97/month. "If I can't get you one job a year I'd go play in traffic."

## KPIs

- 5 hours/day = ~150 calls
- 1-2 booked appointments per day
- 5-10 booked per week
- 3-5 show up per week

## Follow Up System

- Send case study immediately after hanging up
- Text: "Check out this client we helped [X], would love your thoughts"
- Record selfie video if you see their signage
- Keep calling until they say not interested — majority of closes are in the follow up
